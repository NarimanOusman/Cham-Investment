Frequently Asked Questions

What breeds do you offer for export?
+

We specialize in Sudanese, African, Holstein, and Simmental breeds. Each breed is carefully selected for specific purposes including milk production, meat quality, and adaptability to different climates.

How do you ensure the health of exported cattle?
+

Our cattle undergo comprehensive health checks by certified veterinarians before export. We maintain detailed health records and provide all necessary vaccination and health certificates required by destination countries.

What is your typical delivery timeline?
+

Delivery timelines vary based on destination and current inventory. Typically, we can arrange shipment within 2-4 weeks after order confirmation. For urgent requests, we offer expedited options.

Do you handle all export documentation?
+

Yes, we handle all export documentation including health certificates, origin certificates, and customs paperwork. Our team ensures compliance with international regulations to prevent any delays.

Can I visit your facilities before placing an order?
+

Absolutely! We welcome potential clients to visit our facilities. Please contact us to schedule a tour and see firsthand our operations, animal care practices, and quality control measures.
